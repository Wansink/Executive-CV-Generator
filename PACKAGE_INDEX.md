## 💡 KEY DIFFERENTIATORS

This system is different from typical CV templates because:

1. **Evidence-Led, Not Filler-Based**
   - Every claim in the CV traces back to a STAR entry
   - No generic language; specific, quantified results
   - Users can defend every statement in interviews

2. **Role-Specific, Not One-Size-Fits-All**
   - One Master CV + One STAR Library → Many role-specific CVs
   - Each application gets a prioritised CV matching the job posting's requirements
   - ATS-optimised (keywords from job posting)

3. **Interview-Ready**
   - STAR framework used in CVs matches what hiring managers ask
   - Users are prepared to tell their stories when asked "Tell me about a time when..."
   - Stories are credible because they're drawn from documented achievements

4. **Sustainable & Evergreen**
   - Users update Master CV and STAR Library once
   - Subsequent CVs generated in 2 hours (not written from scratch)
   - STAR entries mature over time (users add new achievements quarterly)

## 🎯 TARGET AUDIENCE

**This system works best for:**
- C-suite and senior executives (CIO, CTO, CFO, COO, VP roles)
- Technology leadership roles (>10 years experience)
- Roles in regulated sectors (government, finance, healthcare)
- Candidates with complex, multi-faceted backgrounds
- Career transitions (e.g., private sector → government)

**This system is less ideal for:**
- Junior roles (IC, junior manager, grad schemes)
- Simple, straightforward career progressions
- Industry-specific roles with very standardized formats
- Candidates who prefer a single, static CV
