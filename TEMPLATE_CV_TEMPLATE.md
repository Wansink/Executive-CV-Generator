## NOTES ON COMPLETING THIS TEMPLATE

### Profile Section
- Aim for 3–4 sentences
- Include numbers: years of experience, team size, budget scope, geographic reach
- Include recognition if relevant (awards, lists, board roles)
- Reference regulatory complexity or sector specialisation

### Core Capabilities
- List 10–15 capabilities across leadership and technical domains
- Use bullet points (◆ symbol)
- Avoid generic terms ("good communicator") — be specific ("Stakeholder engagement across hostile environments")

### Professional Experience
- Include every role for the past 15 years
- For recent/significant roles (last 5 years): Include 3–5 key achievements with metrics
- For earlier roles: Summarise in 1–2 sentences
- Always include: Dates, Location, Reporting Line, Team Size, Budget

### Achievements & Responsibilities
- Quantify everything (timelines, percentages, volumes, budget)
- Include stakeholder feedback if available
- Link to business impact, not just activity

### Technical Stack
- Organise by category (Cloud, Security, Data, etc.)
- Include platforms, frameworks, and tools you have *hands-on experience with*
- For leadership roles: Include tools you've selected, implemented, or governed — not just used

### Sector & Organisational Experience
- This helps hiring managers understand your context
- Detail the size and complexity of environments you've worked in
- Name regulatory frameworks or sector-specific challenges you've navigated

## Difference Between Master CV and Role-Specific CV

**Master CV (this document):**
- Complete career record
- All achievements and details
- 3,000+ words typically
- Source material for role-specific CVs

**Role-Specific CV (generated from this + STAR Library + PD):**
- 1,000–1,500 words
- Prioritised by relevance to the role
- Tailored language matching the job posting
- Focused on achievements that demonstrate Key Selection Criteria

One Master CV + One STAR Library = Many role-specific CVs (one per job application)

---

## Maintenance

- **Update annually** — add new roles, achievements, and certifications
- **After major projects** — capture achievements in the STAR Library while fresh
- **Before applications** — ensure accuracy and currentness of all dates, titles, and metrics
